DR. KATHEM TALEBIAN

General Dentist

Dr. Kathem Talebian, DDS, graduated from the University of Baghdad with honours. He also completed a four-year post-doctorate oral surgery training program and received the FDSRCS Diploma.

During his 40+ years of practice, he was a member of various study clubs focusing on periodontal surgery, endodontics, orthodontics, occlusion, and implant dentistry, through which he mastered the skills of interdisciplinary dentistry. He is the founder of the Comprehensive Dentistry study club, and continues to contribute to dental education by mentoring the Oral Surgery for General Dentists and Periodontal Surgery for General Dentists study clubs at UBC.
